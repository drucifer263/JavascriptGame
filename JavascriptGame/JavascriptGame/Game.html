Guessing Game

Welcome to my guessing game.Press the start button to begin. Guess the celebrity in the picture. Make sure to put the first and last name (ex. Hanna West). The first and last Name should be separated with a space and the first letter of each should be CAPITALIZED!! At the end of 10 rounds you will have a bonus round to wager some or all of your points to guess the age of a celebrity within + or - 2 years.

If you are fast and accurate enough you may get a time bonus. Score of 90 or higher plus speed will gain extra points. There are several levels, try them all!!.
